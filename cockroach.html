PERSONAL PROJECT: I am working in a pair with Izzy for my final personal project. We intend to create an interactive quiz/survey that mimics and mocks personality tests and esoteric spirituality that is having a resurgence in this epoch of identity politics. We intend to infuse the survey with our own anxieties for the future regarding careers and personal purpose. We will do this with long winded questions and multiple-choice answers that don’t quite make sense or are surreal/humorous. We want to use p5 sketches, particularly webcam features to ask participants to “reflect on themselves”. We are currently in the process of creating a map of how this “choose your own adventure” will function.

HERE ARE SOME INSPO QUESTIONS/ WORDS/IMAGES ETC: - Illuminati - Cult - Scientology - Myers-Briggs - Personality Test - Star Signs - Humorous - Destiny/ Fate - Uncertainty - Desperation - Loss of identity - Existential - P5s … webcam, scary sounds, sensory experience, “creepy but satirical” - Choose your own adventure style… create a series of outcomes - Outcome… escapism, surreal, stupid, each person is given a career-based outcome or some kind of “destiny”, some people may be asked to retake the quiz.
